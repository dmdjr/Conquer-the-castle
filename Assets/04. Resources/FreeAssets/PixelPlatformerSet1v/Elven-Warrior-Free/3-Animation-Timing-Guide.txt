
===== Animation Timing Guide =====

1. Slice the sprite sheet (See Slicing-Guide.txt for slicing settings)

2. In the Project window, open the sliced sprite sheet

3. Select all frames for one animation (for example, all the idle frames)

4. Drag them directly into the Animation timeline (Unity will automatically place them in order)

5. Select all the frames in the timeline and you will see a blue bar/handle right after the last keyframe

6. Drag the blue bar/handle to the right until the animation length matches the time shown in the table below


Sprite Sheet File Name      | Frames |	Length
==================================================
idle                        | 8      | 1.00 sec
walk                        | 8      | 1.00 sec


⚠️ Do not drag individual keyframes, only adjust the blue bar/handle
